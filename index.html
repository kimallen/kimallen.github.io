About Kim

In broad strokes, I am someone who has a passion for creating and designing things to improve upon the current situation. I like to fix things that are broken, whether it be a bike, our food system, or code. After years of inclining towards innovative solutions in educational programming and garden systems, I have shifted my energies toward technical solutions.

As a recent graduate of Dev Bootcamp, an intensive training program in full stack development, I am most interested in utilizing my programming skills to support social and environmental causes. After years coordinating non-profit programs, supervising youth, and collaborating with fellow organizations, I can easily contribute to a team environment and interpersonal communication. Always ready for a challenge, I bring a drive to learn and openness to new and better ways to perform.

As a multi-passionate person, I can also be found guiding rite-of-passage wilderness programs, coordinating a meditation group, teaching farm ecology to children, pruning fruit trees, rock climbing, knitting dolls for my nephews, or chasing an Ultimate frisbee disc.
